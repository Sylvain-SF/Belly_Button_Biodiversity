Many bacterial species are not well study, and many more are still unknown to science. The purpose of this project is to try to discover bacterial species with abilities to synthesize and taste like beef.At today we are already able to synthesize bacteria from algae, fungi or micro-organism find on plant roots for example, but still search for the elusive bacteria who will provide the perfect taste. New hypothesis show that these bacterias could be found in the human body, more particularly inside the belly button.
